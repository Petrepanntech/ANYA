A COLLECTION OF MOMENTS & THOUGHTS

Morning Coffee

The sun isn't up until you are.
The coffee is bitter,
until your smile sweetens the room.
My day doesn't start with an alarm,
it starts with the quiet sound
of your breathing beside me.

The Map

I used to look at maps
and wonder where I’d go.
Now I look at the lines
on your palms,
and realize I have already arrived.
You are my favorite destination.

Stolen Hoodies

It's three sizes too big.
The sleeves cover your hands,
and you look ridiculous.

But when you wear my clothes,
wrapped in grey cotton,
you look like home.

Gravity

Science says gravity
keeps the planets aligned.
Keeps the moon close.

I don't know about physics,
but I know that whenever
you walk into a room,
my entire world tilts toward you.

Sunday

Doing nothing with you
means everything to me.
Grocery stores,
traffic jams,
rainy afternoons.

You turn the mundane
into magic.
